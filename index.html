Workshops

Get started with Social Media and Branding

Would you like to understand more about Social Media and Branding?

Many entrepeneurs have gone through this. You have found your niche, you believe in your product, but have no clue how to build your brand online.
Whereas historically you could just do your thing without bothering about an online presence, nowadays not having some sort of digital persona makes your audience wonder about the quality of your product.
Purchase decisions are made online, and if you are not online, you are missing out on valuable business.

If you do not consider yourself 'tech-savvy', the idea of getting started can be daunting in itself. Let alone the potential branding errors you can make, pushing your customers away as opposed to attracting them!
Recognizing these fears, I provide support in the form of workshops themed around specific subjects, such as setting up a Facebook page or Blog, choosing the right media outlets and creating marketing material.
I preferably work 1:1 or in very small groups, up to 3 people. This as it allows me to give plenty of attention to each individual and their questions.
